About MentorMentee

MentorMentee is a modern platform designed to facilitate connections between mentors and mentees. Whether you're a student seeking guidance or a mentor ready to share your expertise, our platform provides all the tools you need for effective mentoring relationships.

Features include real-time messaging, meeting scheduling, progress tracking with CGPA monitoring, and a comprehensive dashboard for both students and mentors.
